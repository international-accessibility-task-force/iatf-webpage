IATF Users

The IATF Users are a crucial part of the IATF project. If you want to be an IATF user, you must use assistive technologies in your daily tasks. For instance, a screen reader. IATF users are, ideally: users with blindness and low vision, deafness and hearing loss, limited movement, speech disabilities, photosensitivity, cognitive limitations, elders, and combinations of these. If you are not if none of these groups, but you use assistive technologies in your daily tasks, you might be a good fit.

We will be thrilled if you join our discord, our google group, or our github community as an IATF developer and helps IATF to make technologies accessible for everyone.

You can send accessibility requests for this webpage to admin@internationalaccessibilitytaskforce.com
