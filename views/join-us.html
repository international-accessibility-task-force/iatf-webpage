Join us

You might join us through our Google group, Discord server, or Github organization. The Google groups and Github are public to everyone. The Discord is open to everyone, but you can not read the conversations if you are not on the server. For this reason, we created a new section on the website that logs all the chats in Discord. This way, we are all in sync! If none of these three options fits you, please, email us at: admin@internationalaccessibilitytaskforce.com

You can send accessibility requests for this webpage to admin@internationalaccessibilitytaskforce.com
